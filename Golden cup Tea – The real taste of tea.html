Welcome to Golden Cup Tea!

Our Roots:

Founded in 1991 as Anupam Tea Centre, our journey began in Sumerpur with a vision to share the joy of tea. Now, as Golden Cup Tea Pvt. Ltd., we take pride in our rich heritage and commitment to quality.

Pure Perfection: 

From the lush slopes of Assam, Nilgiri, and Darjeeling, we handpick the finest teas. Our Quality Assurance System guarantees that each cup embodies the highest standards, ensuring an unparalleled experience with every sip.

"In our tea family,
                               Mr. Gheesulal Bhati, our special founder, was like a wise elder who started our amazing tea story. He, along with his brothers—Mr. Lakhmaram Bhati, Mr. Chhogaram Bhati, and Mr. Heeralal Bhati


—went on a journey to share the joy of tea with everyone.

We're really thankful to Mr. Gheesulal for making our tea family so great. His wisdom, like the best tea leaves, made our tea special. Every time we enjoy a cup, it's like saying a quiet thank you to him. His memory is like a friendly guide in our tea moments at

Golden Cup Tea Pvt. Ltd."

Quality Assurance:

Our in-house Quality Assurance System (QAS) ensures that every batch of tea meets the highest standards.

Philosophy of Tea

We believe in the enchantment of the brew, the narratives each sip conveys, and the memories it invokes.

Founder's Legacy:

Our tea legacy was founded in 1991 by Mr. Gheesulal Bhati, a visionary and wise elder.
